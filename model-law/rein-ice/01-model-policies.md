## How to Use These Model Policies

1. **Assess your jurisdiction** - What level of government (city, county, school district)?
2. **Identify champions** - Find elected officials, administrators, or board members willing to sponsor
3. **Adapt the language** - Modify model text for local legal context
4. **Build coalition support** - Engage faith, labor, and community organizations
5. **Anticipate opposition** - Prepare responses to common objections
6. **Plan implementation** - Training, oversight, and accountability mechanisms
