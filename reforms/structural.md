# Structural Reforms

## Overview
Fundamental changes to prevent future democratic backsliding and ensure fair representation for all Americans.

## DC Statehood
- Full voting representation in Congress
- Local control over laws and budget
- End taxation without representation
- Constitutional amendment or admission act

## Puerto Rico Constitutional Status Assembly
- Support self-determination through deliberative process
- Options include:
    - Statehood with full congressional representation
    - Enhanced commonwealth with increased autonomy
    - Free association as sovereign nation with US compact
    - Full independence as sovereign nation
- Binding referendum following assembly recommendations
- Federal support for transition regardless of choice
- Full voting rights during transition period

## Defund ICE - Refund IRS
- Redirect enforcement resources to tax compliance
- Focus on high-income tax avoidance
- Strengthen social services over deportation infrastructure
- Create humane immigration processing system

## Enforce Existing Tax Laws and Fund Cities
- Fully staff IRS enforcement divisions
- Close corporate tax loopholes
- Direct recovered revenue to urban infrastructure
- Support local government capacity building

## Fast-track Pathway to Citizenship
- Clear 5-year path for current residents
- Eliminate arbitrary caps and quotas
- Streamline documentation requirements
- Support integration and civic participation

## Voting Rights Expansion
- Automatic voter registration
- Restore voting rights for returning citizens
- National vote-by-mail standards
- Make Election Day a federal holiday

## Campaign Finance Reform
- Overturn Citizens United through amendment
- Public financing of campaigns
- Strict disclosure requirements
- Limit corporate political spending
